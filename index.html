About Raze

RAZE is a straw-gold non-alcoholic pilsner made for people who train hard but still want a proper beer experience.

It is not an energy drink or a sports drink. It is a crisp, clean, beer-style drink with a balanced hop-forward finish and 0.0% alcohol.

The brand is built around three ideas: hydrate, perform, and focus. It gives athletes a post-game drink that feels premium without slowing them down.
